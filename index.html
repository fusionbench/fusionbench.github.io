FusionBench

A comprehensive Benchmark for QA under Ambiguity and Heterogeneity.

About FusionBench

FusionBench is a comprehensive and complex benchmark designed to evaulate large language models (LLMs) Question Answering (QA) capabilities under both ambiguity and heterogeneity. It has 7,179 QA pairs, with approximately 15.8K text snippets, 137.7K table entries, and 198.1K triples overall.

News

  • Dec. 30, 2025: We officially released the FusionBench dataset on huggingface! FusionBench focues on the ambiguous questions with multi-source information.

Submission

We warmly welcome submissions to our leaderboard. Please refer to the Submission Guidelines below for details, and submit your results as insructed to zcwang@bnu.edu.cn.
